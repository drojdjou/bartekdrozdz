About me

{{/if}}

I have 12 years of experience in coding and designing interactive content.

I've been working in Europe and US for several renowned interactive agencies, including F-i and North Kingdom. Currently I work with Tool of North America in Santa Monica.

The projects I contributed to have been widely recognized in the industry and received multiple awards, including an Emmy, 14 Cannes Lions, an FWA People Choice Award, 3 FWA Sites of the Month, 12 FWA SOTD and 4 FWA Mobile.

In my free time, I like to experiment with web technology and everything around it - from 'cool' technologies, like WebGL and LeapMotion, all the way to CSS and responsive design. Occasionaly I speak at conferences and other events.
