Hello! My name is Bartek.

I am an interactive designer and developer. I live in Los Angeles and I was born in Warsaw, Poland.

With over 20 years of experience in the interactive design, I've had the privilege of working with clients from all over the world, including major ad agencies, Google, Tesla, Disney and many other major brands. My projects have been recognized with prestigious awards, including an Emmy, Cannes Lions and FWAs.

My work has been featured in esteemed publications such as the New York Times, Advertising Age, Fast Company, Creativity Magazine, Forbes, and Fortune.

I bring a diverse range of skills to the table. From interactive design, front-end and back-end programming to cloud computing, I thrive in the intersection of technology and creativity. This blend of technical expertise and artistic vision allows me to craft immersive and captivating digital experiences.
