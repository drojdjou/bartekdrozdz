Earth model with multiple textures and geo-location.

This model of the earth will show you where you are (if you are unsure...). It also uses a shader with multiple textures to render night map, day map, a normal map and some clouds.

Note: Be sure to click Allow on the toolbar above to share your location with the site.

Technology: WebGL, GeoLocation API

You need a WebGL enabled browser run this experiment. Chrome or Firefox are the best choice. You can also enable WebGL on Safari on desktop.
