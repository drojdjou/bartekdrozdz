Earth model with multiple textures and geolocation.

This model of the earth will show you where you are (if you are unsure about that...). It also uses a shader with multiple textures to render a day & night map along with a normal/bump map and some clouds.

Note: Be sure to click Allow on the toolbar above to share your location.

Technology: WebGL, Geolocation API

You need a WebGL enabled browser run this experiment. Chrome or Firefox are the best choice. You can also enable WebGL on Safari on desktop.
